Who are we ?

ProDonation is an online donation platform which collects donation and does charity to help and mark a social impact. We believe, all human beings must have the same opportunity to succeed in life, irrespective of the circumstances they were born into.

We focus on :

  • Rehabilitating and providing education, shelter and food to economically backward, child labourers, disabled and orphans
  • Providing relief and rehabilitation to the victims of Natural Calamities like floods and droughts
  • Enlightening and educating the indigenous people and remote rural masses

    • Our vision :

    To alleviate poverty by enabling the world to give

    Our values :

  • Integrity in everything we do
  • Serve with passion
  • Empathy for all
